About Us

This is a webpage designed from 5 graduate students about an online library. With the advancement of technology, having a system that can manage the books of the library is very beneficial for a lot of reasons. One of them is being more organized and keeping track of the books in real time.

This website will help librarians to manage their books.

Keep it simple !

We are driven to make the book collection easier and more joyful. We create the best book collection for user and provide a pleasant, uncomplicated online library.
